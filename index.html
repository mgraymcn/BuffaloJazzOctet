Buffalo Jazz Octet

“a veritable who’s who list of killer regional jazz players” - Jeff Miers, The Buffalo News

Photo by Megan Metté

The Buffalo Jazz Octet has performed since 2011, combining the power and compositional intricacy of a large ensemble with the flexibility and dynamism of a small improvising combo. The band’s players are accomplished in both mainstream jazz music and the avant-garde. Besides concert performances at the 2014 and 2015 Queen City Jazz Festivals, Pausa Art House, Villa Maria College, Hallwalls Contemporary Arts Center, and Jazz@Unity, the band also accompanied the silent film Phantom of the Opera with all-original music in October of 2012. The Octet has also performed with special guest musicians Joe Ford (alto and soprano saxophone) and Esin Gunduz (voice).

  • Kelly Bucheger, alto and tenor saxophones
  • Nelson Rivera, tenor and soprano saxophones
  • Steve Baczkowski, baritone saxophone and more
  • Tim Clarke, trumpet and flugelhorn
  • Phil Sims, trombone
  • Michael McNeill, piano
  • Dave Arenius or Brian DeJesus, bass
  • John Bacon, drums and percussion
  • Conducted by Brendan Fitzgerald
